# Use Cases

## Overview

Chronicle / Legacy Protocol is designed as a conceptual protocol memory layer. Its use cases focus on preserving contribution, knowledge, reputation, and historical context rather than issuing rewards or deploying implementation code.

## 1. Contributor Memory

A decentralized ecosystem can use Chronicle to preserve a structured history of contributor work across repositories, governance forums, documentation hubs, and community spaces.

Potential value:

- Helps new participants understand who contributed to what
- Preserves long-term documentation and infrastructure work
- Reduces loss of institutional memory
- Supports contributor continuity across time

## 2. Governance Context

Governance proposals often depend on historical context. Chronicle could help preserve the reasoning behind decisions, the contributors involved, the debates that shaped outcomes, and the lessons learned after implementation.

Potential value:

- Improves proposal review
- Preserves decision history
- Helps future voters understand prior trade-offs
- Reduces repeated governance debates caused by lost context

## 3. Knowledge Inheritance

Chronicle could help transfer knowledge from experienced contributors to new participants through archives, decision records, contributor notes, and AI-assisted summaries.

Potential value:

- Faster onboarding
- Better maintainer handoffs
- Reduced dependency on private chats
- Preservation of operational lessons

## 4. Reputation-Aware Contribution Review

A contextual reputation layer could help identify contributors with relevant experience in documentation, governance, infrastructure, research, or security.

Potential value:

- Better reviewer selection
- More informed grant evaluation
- Category-specific trust signals
- Reduced reliance on social visibility alone

## 5. Ecosystem Research

Researchers could use archived contribution records to study how ecosystems evolve, which forms of contribution are most durable, and how governance or documentation maturity develops over time.

Potential value:

- Longitudinal ecosystem analysis
- Contributor retention studies
- Documentation maturity tracking
- Governance participation research

## 6. AI-Assisted Ecosystem Mentorship

An AI mentor layer could help users navigate archived knowledge, understand historical decisions, and find relevant contribution pathways.

Potential value:

- Source-linked guidance
- Better onboarding
- Easier knowledge discovery
- Continuity between generations of contributors

## Use Case Boundaries

Chronicle should not be used as:

- A reward farming engine
- A universal social ranking system
- A replacement for governance voting
- A transferable reputation market
- A centralized authority over contributor value

## Research Position

The strongest use cases preserve memory and context. The weakest use cases are those that convert contribution into simple scores or speculative incentives.
